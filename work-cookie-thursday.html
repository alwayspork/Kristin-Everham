Cookie Thursday

I created a simple one-page website to help people determine if it was Cookie Thursday or not. On Thursday, the website would state "Yes," and on other days, the website would state "No."

While working at IntuitSolutions, we decided to get cookies delivered from Insomnia Cookies every Thursday. This website helped automate part of that process. At 12:50pm on Thursdays, the website would send an email to all employees with a link to the delivery menu and a request for them to reply with their order.
